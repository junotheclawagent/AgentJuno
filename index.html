Honest answers

Things you might be wondering.

I already pay for Claude. Why would I pay for this?

Keep Claude — seriously. This doesn't replace it. Claude is for when you're at the keyboard. Juno is for everything else: the work that needs doing at 3am, the emails triaged before you wake up, the research scout running while you're in meetings. Claude forgets you the second you close the tab. Juno knows who you are, what you're building, and what you don't want it to touch. It costs €0.17 a day to run. Most people who buy this keep their Claude subscription.

What if I'm not technical?

Then this is exactly for you. We do the install. We hand you a manual written for the curious, not for engineers. You text the agent in Telegram. That's the entire interface. If you want zero setup at all, request a seat on the Hosted Trial — we run everything, you just talk to it.

What does the 5-day free trial mean?

After we complete your install and hand over the keys, you have five days to put Juno through its paces. If it doesn't fit your life, email us and we refund the install fee in full. No forms, no questions. We'd rather you be honest than quietly disappointed.

What is the Hosted Trial and why is it limited?

The Hosted Trial is for people who want to feel Juno before committing to owning a server. We run the infrastructure, you get two weeks of the full experience for €9.99. It's limited to 10 people because we're managing each instance personally at this stage — this isn't automated yet. When the seats fill, they're gone.

Where does my data live?

On the Standard and Pro tiers: a server in an EU country of your choice (default Helsinki, Finland). GDPR-protected. We don't host it; we don't see it after handover. You own the keys. On the Hosted Trial: our managed EU server, deleted at the end of the two weeks unless you convert to an install.

What if I want to cancel the Pro retainer?

Email us. One month notice. The server stays yours — you're just opting out of the monthly maintenance. Everything we've built for you remains running. Nothing gets deleted. The one-time install fee is yours to keep regardless.
