Honest answers

Things you might be wondering.

I already pay for Claude. Why would I pay for this?

Keep Claude, seriously. This doesn't replace it. Claude is for when you're at the keyboard. Juno is for everything else: the work that needs doing at 3am, the emails triaged before you wake up, the research running while you're in meetings. Claude forgets you the second you close the tab. Juno knows who you are, what you're building, and what you don't want it to touch. It costs about €0.20 a day to run. Most people who buy this keep their Claude subscription.

What if I'm not technical?

Then this is exactly for you. I do the install. I hand you a manual written for the curious, not for engineers. You text the agent in Telegram. That's the entire interface. If you want zero setup at all, request a seat on the Hosted Trial, where I run everything and you just talk to it.

What can Juno actually do — and what can't it?

Juno handles text, research, drafting, scheduling, web search, inbox triage, and task automation over Telegram. It cannot generate images, render graphics, or produce any visual files. This is not a limitation we're working around — the server it runs on is a lightweight EU VPS with no graphics capability, by design. If you ask Juno to make a carousel, a poster, or any visual asset, it will tell you it can't, give you the copy and creative direction, and tell you to take that into Claude.ai, Midjourney, or Canva to execute. That's the correct workflow, not a workaround.

What's the refund policy?

Any time before I hand over the keys, you can walk away and get refunded in full, minus a €15 admin fee for the intake work. Once it's handed over, the work is done, the agent is configured to you, and you're holding the keys. It's yours. If you want to try before committing to an install, the €9.99 Hosted Trial is the way to do that.

What is the Hosted Trial and why is it limited?

The Hosted Trial is for people who want to feel Juno before committing to owning a server. I run the infrastructure, you get two weeks of the full experience for €9.99. It's limited to 10 people because I'm managing each instance personally at this stage, and this isn't automated yet. When the seats fill, they're gone.

Where does my data live?

On the Hardened Setup and Pro tiers: a server in an EU country of your choice (default Helsinki, Finland). GDPR-protected. I don't host it, and I don't see it after handover. You own the keys. On the Hosted Trial: a managed EU server I run, deleted at the end of the two weeks unless you convert to an install.

What if I want to cancel the Pro retainer?

Email me. One month notice. The server stays yours. You're just opting out of the monthly maintenance. Everything built for you remains running. Nothing gets deleted. The one-time install fee is yours to keep regardless.
