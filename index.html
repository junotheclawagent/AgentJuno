What this is not

OpenClaw Hardened is not a SaaS product. We don't host anything for you. We don't see your data. We don't keep credentials after handover. The server is yours, the keys are yours, and we walk away once it works.

It's also not a magic productivity hack. It's a tool you'll learn to use over a few weeks. The first three days you'll feel like you're not using it right. By the second week, you'll feel a quiet relief.

If you want a chatbot, use ChatGPT. If you want an autonomous agent you can trust with non-trivial work, that lives on infrastructure you control, that refuses things it shouldn't do even when you push — keep reading.

The principles

  • Less, but tested We install almost nothing speculatively. Every skill is vetted by hand. Every config decision is documented. The product is restraint.
  • EU by default Your data, your server, your jurisdiction. GDPR-protected from day one.
  • The friction is the feature The agent asks before doing things that matter. That's not annoyance. That's safety with stakes attached.
  • You own it; you operate it This isn't a magic device. It's a tool you check in on for five minutes a week. We handed you the keys; we're not your IT department unless you put us on retainer.
